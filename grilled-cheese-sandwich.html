Grilled cheese sandwich

A grilled cheese sandwich offers a perfect balance of crispy, buttery bread and melted, gooey cheese. The outer crust is golden and crunchy, while the inside is warm and savory. Each bite delivers a satisfying combination of rich, creamy cheese and a slight hint of toasted flavor.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

6 min.

serving

1-2 servings

Tags :

bread cheese grilled cheese sandwich

instructions

step 1

Butter one side of each slice of bread. Place a slice of cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread.

step 2

Heat a frying pan or non-stick pan over medium heat. Place the sandwich in the pan, buttered side down, and cook for 2-3 minutes, until golden brown. Flip and grill the other side for another 2-3 minutes.

step 3

Remove from the pan, slice in half, and serve immediately while warm and gooey. Enjoy!

ingredients

2 slices of bread

2 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)

1-2 tbsp butter

Utensils

Frying Pan or Non-Stick Pan

Spatula and Butter Knife

Measuring Spoon (for butter)

Knife (for slicing the sandwich)
