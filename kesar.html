kesar badam milk

Kesar Badam Milk is a rich, creamy beverage with a delicate balance of flavors. The sweetness of milk pairs beautifully with the nutty aroma of finely ground almonds, while saffron adds a subtle floral note and golden hue. Each sip is soothing, fragrant, and luxuriously indulgent.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags :

kesar badam milk drink

instructions

step 1

Boil the macaroni in salted water until al dente, then drain and set aside.

step 2

In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute. Slowly add milk, whisking constantly, and cook until thickened. Stir in shredded cheese, salt, and pepper until smooth.

step 3

Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce, mixing until evenly coated. Serve immediately for creamy, cheesy perfection!

ingredients

2 cups of elbow macaroni, milk & shredded cheddar cheese

2 tbsp of butter and all-purpose flour

1/2 tsp salt & 1/4 tsp pepper

Utensils

Medium pot

Saucepan

Whisk, spoon, or spatula

Measuring cups and a colander.
