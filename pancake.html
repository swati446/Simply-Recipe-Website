Banana Pancakes

Banana pancakes have a soft, fluffy texture with a rich, sweet flavor. The natural sweetness of ripe bananas melds with a hint of vanilla and warm, buttery notes. Each bite is comforting, with the fruitâ€™s subtle tang balancing the richness of the batter for a delightful, indulgent taste.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags :

banana breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

In a bowl, mash 2 ripe bananas. Mix in 1 cup flour, 1/2 cup milk, 1 egg, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until smooth.

step 2

Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan, cooking each pancake for 2-3 minutes on each side, until golden brown.

step 3

Stack pancakes, drizzle with syrup or honey, and add optional toppings like sliced bananas, berries, or nuts. Enjoy!

ingredients

2 ripe bananas, mashed

1 cup flour, 1/2 cup milk, 1 egg, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp vanilla extract, pinch of salt

Optional toppings: syrup, honey, sliced bananas, berries, or nuts
