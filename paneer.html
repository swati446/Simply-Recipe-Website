Shahi Paneer

Shahi Paneer offers a luxurious taste, blending creamy richness with aromatic spices. The velvety gravy combines tomatoes, cashews, and cream, enhanced by fragrant cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. Soft paneer cubes absorb the flavorful curry, creating a mildly sweet, savory, and slightly tangy dish.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags :

paneer gravy food cream

instructions

step 1

Blend 2 tomatoes, 10 cashews, and 1 green chili into a smooth paste.

step 2

Heat oil/butter, sauté 1 tsp cumin, 2 cardamoms, and 1 bay leaf then Add 1 finely chopped onion, cook until golden.

step 3

Stir in the tomato-cashew paste, cook until oil separates.Mix in 1 tsp coriander, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, and salt.

step 4

Add ½ cup water, simmer for 5 minutes. Stir in ¼ cup cream for richness. Add 200g paneer cubes, simmer gently for 5 minutes.

step 5

Sprinkle fresh coriander and add some cream. Serve with naan or rice and ENJOY!

ingredients

Paneer - 200g (cubed), Tomatoes - 2, Cashews - 10, Green chili - 1, Onion - 1 (finely chopped), Cream - ¼ cup

Oil/Butter - 2 tbsp,Cumin seeds - 1 tsp, Cardamoms - 2 (green), Coriander powder - 1 tsp, Turmeric powder - ½ tsp, Red chili powder - 1 tsp, Salt - To taste

Fresh coriander leaves - For garnish

Utensils

Blender/Grinder

Knife and Cutting Board

Deep Pan/Kadai

Spatula and Measuring Spoons
