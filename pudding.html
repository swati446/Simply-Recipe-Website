Pudding

Pudding is a creamy, velvety dessert that melts in your mouth with each spoonful. Its rich, smooth texture is perfectly balanced with a delicate sweetness. Whether flavored with vanilla, chocolate, or caramel, it delivers a comforting, luscious taste that’s both indulgent and satisfying, leaving a lingering, delightful sweetness.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

30-35 min.

serving

4-5 servings

Tags :

pudding dessert caramel sweet

instructions

step 1

Heat 1 tbsp ghee, add 500g grated carrots, and cook for 5 minutes. Add 500ml milk, simmer until absorbed (15-20 minutes).

step 2

Mix in 4 tbsp sugar and 1 tbsp ghee, cook until glossy. Add ½ tsp cardamom powder and mix well.

step 3

Top with chopped nuts and raisins. Serve warm or chilled.

ingredients

500g carrots (grated), 500ml full-fat milk and 4 tbsp sugar

2 tbsp ghee and ½ tsp cardamom powder

2 tbsp chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios), 1 tbsp raisins

Utensils

Grater and Large pan

Spatula and Measuring cups

Serving bowl
