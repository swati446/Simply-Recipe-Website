Vegetable Soup

Vegetable soup is a comforting blend of earthy, savory, and slightly sweet flavors. The broth is warm and rich, infused with the freshness of herbs like parsley or thyme. The vegetables add layers of texture—tender carrots, hearty potatoes, and crisp celery—creating a harmonious medley of natural, wholesome goodness

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

4-6 servings

Tags :

vegetable breakfast soup food

instructions

step 1

Chop assorted vegetables (e.g., carrots, celery, potatoes, onions, and tomatoes). Mince garlic and gather herbs like thyme or parsley.

step 2

Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil. Add vegetables, seasoning with salt, pepper, and herbs. Pour in vegetable broth, bringing to a boil.

step 3

Reduce heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes until vegetables are tender. Adjust seasoning, garnish with fresh herbs, and enjoy warm!

ingredients

Base Ingredients:1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 onion (chopped), 2 garlic cloves (minced)

Vegetables:2 carrots (diced), 2 celery stalks (sliced), 2 potatoes (cubed), 1 cup tomatoes (diced)

Seasoning and Broth:4 cups vegetable broth, Salt and pepper (to taste), 1 teaspoon thyme or parsley (optional), Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Utensils

Knife and Cutting board

Large pot & spatula for cooking

Ladle & Soup bowls

Peeler
